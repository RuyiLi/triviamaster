About

TriviaMaster is an open-source trivia bot for Discord. It's incredibly easy to use, and has over 160,000 questions from open-source trivia APIs.

Commands

Your guild may use a different prefix with TriviaMaster! To check the prefix that your guild is using with TriviaMaster, type @TriviaMaster prefix. The default prefix for TriviaMaster is t.

Usage arguments that are wrapped in [square brackets] are optional, and arguments that are wrapped in (parentheses) are required.
